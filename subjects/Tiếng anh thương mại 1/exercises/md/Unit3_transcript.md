> **Host**: Have they got anything lined up for this year?

> **Andy**: With a normal roller coaster you are sitting on a seat with the track under you. With a wing coaster, the seats kind of stick out at the side. So the passengers have nothing below or above them. You feel as if you're flying.
> **Host**: So it's more frightening?
> **Andy**: More exciting. Way more exciting.
>
> **Host**: Can you tell us about Asian parks?
